Welcome to K.D.Public School

K.D.PUBLIC SCHOOL is an English Medium, Co-Educational School which was established by Mr. Dilip Kumar. The School is upto VIIIth class.

Welcome to KD PUBLIC SCHOOL where learning is a fun and a wonderful experience. A good school is the need for the betterment of society and we are trying our best.We have a very ultra modern infrastructure, Wi-Fi campus, smart classes and very lucid environment for holistic development of our children.

Our school is imparting value based education which is the need of hour. KD Public School aims at the harmonious development like intellectual, physical, emotional, moral and spiritual development of a child.

To Produce Global Talents

Some times ago, there were not good english medium schools in our area. So to provide better education, parents needed to shift in urban area to sustain their kids for present competitive environment.

We started this organization with the same mission and vission that parents can avail the same quality education here for which they needed to shift in Urban Area.

We follow NCERT Pattern Text Books. We follow the continuous and comprehensive evaluation (CCE) guidelines laid down by Uttar Pradesh State Council of Educational Research and Training Continuous Assessment tools help to evaluate learns depth of learning.

Smart Class rooms, highly proficient native faculty, and visiting staff balanced curriculum with ample space for co-curricular activities, religious and value based education etc. make our schooling experience a unique one.

In addition to the prescribed syllabus Music, Craft, Drawing, Painting, Clay Modeling, Yoga, News Paper Reading, Computer etc. has also been included in the school curriculum.
