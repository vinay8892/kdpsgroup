Admission

Admission of new student shall be made before the beginning of the academic session. No amount of fees shall be refunded even if a pupil for any reason leaves school immediately after the admission. Student selection is based on academic and non-academic considerations. Admission to any classes are on the basic of written test and performance in the previous schools
